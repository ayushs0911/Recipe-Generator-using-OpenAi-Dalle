

Recipe Title: Creamy Cheesy Pasta with Corn and Capsicum

Ingredients: 
-1 onion, diced 
-Â½ capsicum, diced 
-1 cup of corn
-250g pasta of choice
-2 tablespoons of olive oil
-1 can of cream of mushroom soup
-2 cups of grated cheese

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 350F/180C.

2. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add in the onion and capsicum and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

3. Add in the corn and cook until lightly browned, about 3 minutes.

4. Add the cream of mushroom soup and stir until combined. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.

5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions.

6. Once the pasta is cooked, drain and return to the pot.

7. Add the sauce to the pasta and stir to combine.

8. Transfer the pasta into a greased baking dish.
