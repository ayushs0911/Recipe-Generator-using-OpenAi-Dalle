

Recipe Title: Spicy Paneer Tomato Curry

Ingredients:
- 1 cup paneer, cubed
- 2 large tomatoes, chopped
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 cup curd
- 1 capsicum, diced
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Once the oil is hot, add the cumin seeds and cook for a few seconds until they start to sizzle.
3. Add the onions and cook until they are translucent.
4. Add the tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
5. Add the capsicum, turmeric powder, garam masala, red chilli powder and coriander powder.
6. Cook for a few minutes until the spices are fragrant.
7. Add the curd and stir until it is completely mixed in.
8. Add the paneer cubes and mix gently.
