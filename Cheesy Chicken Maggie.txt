

Recipe Title: Cheesy Chicken Maggie

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cheese maggie
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 pound chicken, cubed

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the cheese maggie, diced tomato, and diced onion.
3. Add the cubed chicken to the bowl and mix until all ingredients are combined.
4. Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan with cooking spray.
5. Pour the mixture into the greased baking pan and spread it out evenly.
6. Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
